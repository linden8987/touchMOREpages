About

touchMORE is a high-level emulator for iPhone OS apps, forked from touchHLE. Written in Rust, it takes the place of iPhone OS itself, providing clean-room implementations of system frameworks like UIKit, Foundation, and OpenGL ES.

  • Supports apps for iPhone OS 2.x and 3.0.
  • Working toward 64-bit iOS support.

Platform Support

Officially supported with binary releases:

  • Windows (x64)
  • macOS (x64/Apple Silicon via Rosetta)
  • Android (AArch64)

Usage

Place your decrypted .ipa files or .app bundles in the touchHLE_apps directory. Use the built-in app picker to launch your games.

Command Line

.\touchHLE.exe "YourGame.ipa" --options

Special Android Notes

Due to Scoped Storage, place files in:
/sdcard/Android/data/org.touchhle.android/files/touchHLE_apps

Local Multiplayer

Limited Wi-Fi multiplayer is supported in titles like Asphalt 4 and N.O.V.A. Ensure --allow-network-access is enabled.
